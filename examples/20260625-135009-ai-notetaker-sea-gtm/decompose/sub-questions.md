# Sub-questions

Objective: Assess the market opportunity for an AI sales-call notetaker targeting SMB sales teams in Southeast Asia, with a focus on Vietnam and Indonesia.

| ID | Sub-question | Claim type |
|---|---|---|
| SQ-01 | Who are the leading AI meeting/sales-call notetaker vendors and how does each position itself (target segment, headline capability)? | competitive |
| SQ-02 | What do the leading AI sales-call notetaker vendors charge, and what is the entry price point for an SMB-sized team? | competitive |
| SQ-03 | What does the research literature report about the reliability (word error rate / accuracy) of automatic speech recognition / transcription systems? | technical |
| SQ-04 | What does the research literature report about the reliability and known failure modes of automatic (LLM/abstractive) meeting or conversation summarization? | technical |
| SQ-05 | What is the size of the business/enterprise base (number of registered firms or business density) in Vietnam and Indonesia that an SMB sales tool could address? | quantitative |
| SQ-06 | What is the recent macroeconomic context (GDP and population) for Vietnam and Indonesia that frames the addressable market? | quantitative |
| SQ-07 | What is the published market size (TAM/SAM) specifically for the SMB sales-call AI notetaker category in Southeast Asia? | quantitative |
