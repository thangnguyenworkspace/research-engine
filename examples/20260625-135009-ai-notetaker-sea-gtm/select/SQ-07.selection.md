# Selection — SQ-07

**Sub-question:** What is the published market size (TAM/SAM) specifically for the SMB sales-call AI notetaker category in Southeast Asia?
**Claim type:** quantitative
**Deterministic trigger:** quantitative market-size claim. This is a category market-size (TAM/SAM) figure, NOT a country-level development statistic — so the World Bank deterministic trigger does NOT fire (it owns no such series). No catalog source is the primary owner of a private-market-research TAM figure.

## Candidates considered

| Source | Fit-for-claim | Verifiability | Access tier | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| World Bank | low — does not publish category/private-market TAM figures; "AI notetaker market size in SEA" is not a development indicator | n/a — series does not exist | open | rejected |
| OpenAlex | low — scholarly graph does not catalogue commercial market-sizing reports | low — wrong modality | open | rejected |
| Web (WebSearch/WebFetch) | low-medium — market-research firms (Grand View, Gartner, etc.) publish category TAMs, but a *SMB sales-call notetaker, SEA-specific* figure is almost never free; open-web hits are press releases citing paywalled reports for a broader "AI meeting/transcription" category, not this narrow SMB-SEA slice | low — figures behind paywalls; free snippets are derivative, methodologically opaque, and scope-mismatched (global or broad-category, not SMB-SEA notetaker) | open | chosen (best-available, expected thin) |

## Chosen

- **Web (WebSearch/WebFetch)** — trust: context-only. Will attempt to find any published TAM/SAM for the category; queried by WebSearch for the SEA AI-notetaker / meeting-transcription market size. Trust is capped at context-only: any free figure will be a derivative press-release number for a broader category, not the specific SMB-SEA notetaker slice. If nothing scoped is found, the sub-question is recorded as unanswered rather than back-filled with a mismatched number.

## Rejected

- **World Bank** — owns no category-market-size series; the quantitative trigger fires only for country-level development statistics, which this is not.
- **OpenAlex** — does not index commercial market-sizing reports.

## Honest scope note

The v1 free stack (Web + OpenAlex + World Bank) has no primary owner of a private-market TAM. Credible category sizing lives in paid analyst reports (Gartner, IDC, Grand View, MarketsandMarkets), which are out of the keyless v1 catalog. The expected outcome is that this sub-question comes back thin or empty; that gap is recorded honestly rather than filled with a fabricated figure.
