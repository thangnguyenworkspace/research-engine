# Selection — SQ-02

**Sub-question:** What do the leading AI sales-call notetaker vendors charge, and what is the entry price point for an SMB-sized team?
**Claim type:** competitive
**Deterministic trigger:** competitive / open-web pricing claim → built-in Web tools. Pricing is published on vendor pricing pages and pricing round-ups; the primary owner of a price is the vendor's own page.

## Candidates considered

| Source | Fit-for-claim | Verifiability | Access tier | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Web (WebSearch/WebFetch) | high — vendor pricing pages are the primary owner of the price; comparison pages aggregate them | medium-high — the vendor's own pricing page is primary and dated by access; third-party round-ups are derivative and can lag | open | chosen |
| World Bank | low — no commercial pricing data | low — wrong modality | open | rejected |
| OpenAlex | low — no commercial pricing data | low — wrong modality | open | rejected |

## Chosen

- **Web (WebSearch/WebFetch)** — trust: corroborating (vendor pricing pages are primary for their own list price, but list prices change frequently, so treat the retrieved figure as accurate-as-of-access-date). Will capture entry/SMB tier pricing; queried by WebSearch for current notetaker pricing, then WebFetch on a pricing page or a recent pricing comparison.

## Rejected

- **World Bank** — wrong claim type; country statistics only.
- **OpenAlex** — wrong claim type; scholarly works only.
