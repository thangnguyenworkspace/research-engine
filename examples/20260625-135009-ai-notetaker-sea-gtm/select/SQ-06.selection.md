# Selection — SQ-06

**Sub-question:** What is the recent macroeconomic context (GDP and population) for Vietnam and Indonesia that frames the addressable market?
**Claim type:** quantitative
**Deterministic trigger:** quantitative country-level statistic → World Bank (the primary owner of GDP and population indicators, keyed by country and year).

## Candidates considered

| Source | Fit-for-claim | Verifiability | Access tier | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| World Bank | high — GDP (current US$, NY.GDP.MKTP.CD) and total population (SP.POP.TOTL) are first-class World Bank indicators keyed by country and year | high — primary owner; exact, dated, re-queryable by stable indicator code | open | chosen |
| Web (WebSearch/WebFetch) | medium — the figures are findable on the open web | low — any page is a derivative restating the World Bank/IMF number, often without the reporting year | open | rejected |

## Chosen

- **World Bank** — trust: primary. Answers GDP and population for both countries directly; queried at `https://api.worldbank.org/v2/country/VNM/indicator/NY.GDP.MKTP.CD?format=json`, `.../SP.POP.TOTL`, and the IDN equivalents, taking the most recent non-null year for each.

## Rejected

- **Web (WebSearch/WebFetch)** — would only restate the World Bank/IMF figure; using a derivative for a primary-owned statistic loses the reporting year and exact value. Reserved as fallback only if the API is unreachable.
