**Fireflies.ai (vendor pricing page, primary for its own list price), as accessed 2026-06-25:**
- **Free** — $0. Unlimited transcription, unlimited AI summaries, 400 mins storage/team, 20 AI credits.
- **Pro** — $10/seat/month billed annually ($18/month if billed monthly). Adds video recording, downloads, Personal Assistant, AI Skills, Voice Agents, action items/task manager, unlimited integrations.
- **Business** (marked "Most Popular") — $19/seat/month billed annually ($29/month monthly). Adds unlimited storage, 30 AI credits, multi-language mode, conversation intelligence, team analytics.
- **Enterprise** — $39/seat/month (annual billing only). Adds 50 AI credits, rules engine, SSO+SCIM, audit logs, HIPAA, private storage.

**Corroborating cross-vendor pricing from the WebSearch digest (derivative round-ups):**
- **Otter** — Pro $10/seat/mo (annual), Business $19/seat/mo (annual), Enterprise $39/seat/mo; free tier capped at 300 mins/month. (Mirrors Fireflies' tier prices.)
- **Fathom** — Premium ~$15/user/mo (annual) / $19 monthly; Team Edition ~$19 (annual) / $29 monthly; Team Edition Pro ~$29 (annual) / $39 monthly. Most generous free tier (unlimited recordings/transcription; AI summaries capped at 5 meetings/month free).

Entry paid price point for an SMB team clusters at ~$10/seat/month (annual) for Fireflies and Otter Pro; Fathom's lowest paid tier is ~$15/user/month (annual).
