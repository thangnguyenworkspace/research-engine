## Material

**Fathom pricing page (fathom.ai/pricing), accessed 2026-06-25 (vendor-primary):**

- **Free** — $0/month per user. Unlimited recordings + transcriptions; instant AI call summaries; clips, playlists, and search across calls.
- **Premium** (individual) — **$20/month, or $16/month billed annually**. Adds advanced call summaries (15+ templates), AI-generated action items, conversational meeting assistant, custom meeting bot.
- **Team** — **$19/user/month, or $15/user/month billed annually** (minimum 2 users). Adds global search across shared calls, highlight playlists, comments/folders/keyword alerts, custom transcription vocabulary, SSO.
- **Business** — **$34/user/month, or $25/user/month billed annually** (minimum 2 users). Adds CRM field sync, Deal View, coaching metrics/AI scorecards, custom data-retention policies.

## Correction vs cycle-1 derivative figures

Cycle-1's digest claimed Fathom "Premium ~$15/user/mo annual (~$19 monthly); Team ~$19 annual; Team Edition Pro ~$29 annual." The vendor page differs:
- Fathom's **lowest paid tier is Premium at $16/mo annual ($20 monthly)** — not ~$15/$19.
- Tier names are **Premium / Team / Business** (no "Team Edition Pro").
- Team is **$15/user/mo annual ($19 monthly)**; Business is **$25/user/mo annual ($34 monthly)**.
- The free tier is confirmed: $0 with unlimited recordings + transcriptions.

List prices change frequently — figures are accurate-as-of access date 2026-06-25.
