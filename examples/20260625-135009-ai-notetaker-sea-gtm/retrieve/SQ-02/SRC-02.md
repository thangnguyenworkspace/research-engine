## Why this file exists

Cycle-1 partialled CLM-10/CLM-12: Otter's prices were cited to the Fireflies pricing fetch (SQ-02/SRC-01), which carries no Otter pricing. This file fetches Otter's own pricing page so the Otter figures are grounded to Otter's own source — and corrects the stale derivative numbers in the process.

## Material

**Otter.ai pricing page, accessed 2026-06-25 (vendor-primary):**

- **Basic** — "Free forever." 300 monthly transcription minutes; 3 lifetime audio/video file imports.
- **Pro** — **$8.33/user/month billed annually** (page: "save 51%"); **$16.99/user/month** billed monthly. 1,200 in-app recording minutes; up to 90 mins/meeting; 10 monthly file imports.
- **Business** — **$19.99/user/month billed annually** (page: "save 33%"); $30/user/month regular monthly, shown with a $24/user/month promotional monthly price. Unlimited meetings + in-app recordings; up to 4 hours/meeting; unlimited file imports.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing (requires a demo). "Everything in Business, plus" enterprise security, SSO/SCIM, API access, unlimited custom AI workflows.

## Correction vs cycle-1 derivative figures

Cycle-1's digest claimed Otter Pro $10 / Business $19 / Enterprise $39 (mirroring Fireflies). The vendor page does NOT support that:
- Otter Pro is **$8.33/user/mo annual** (not $10).
- Otter Business is **$19.99/user/mo annual** (~$20, not exactly $19).
- Otter Enterprise is **custom-quote**, not a published $39 (the $39 figure was a mis-transcription from Fireflies' Enterprise tier).
- The free-tier "300 minutes" cap is the one digest figure that the vendor page corroborates (300 monthly transcription minutes), though as a per-month transcription cap.

List prices change frequently — figures are accurate-as-of access date 2026-06-25.
